Monuments & Memorials

  1. White House
    1600 Pensylvania Ave NW, Washington D.C. 20500
    Tours must be set up between 14 to 90 days prior to your desired tour day. Tours are free of charge. Requests for tours must go through your member of congress.
  2. Arlington National Cemetary
    Arlington, VA 22211
    Car parking is $2/hour, bus parking is $8/hour
    The visitors center offers guided walking tours and trolley tours. Trolley tours are $12/adult $9/senior $6/child. You are also welcome to self-tour at your leisure.
    Photographs are welcome, but please refrain from capturing funerals and loved ones visiting grave sites.
  3. Washington Monument
    2 15th NW, Washignton D.C. 20024
    Open 7 days a week from 9am-5pm. You can get free tickets to tour at the visitor center. There are limited spots per tour.
  4. Lincoln Memorial
    2 Lincoln Memorial Cir NW, Washington D.C. 20037
    Open 24/7

  7. The Pentagon
    Washington D.C. 22202
    Tours from Monday-Thursday 10am-4pm and Friday from 12pm-4pm. Must reserve tours between 14 to 90 days in advance. ABSOLUTELY NO PHOTOS
  8. National WWII Memorial
    1750 Independence Ave SW, Washington D.C. 20024
    Open 24/7
  9. Library of Congress
    10 First Street S.E. Washington, DC 20540
    Tours available Monday–Saturday 8:30 a.m.–4:30 p.m.
    Groups of 10 or more need to make advanced reservations. Groups smaller than 10, go to the information desk to set up a same day tour and arrive 30 mins before your tour time. You will be going thorough airport-like security. All weapons are prohibited. You can see all of the guidlines to visit the library here.
